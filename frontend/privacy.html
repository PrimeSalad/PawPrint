Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection

At PawPrint AI, we prioritize your privacy. The primary data we collect includes the images you upload for canine breed classification. These images are processed by our neural network purely for analysis.

2. Use of Images

Uploaded images are held temporarily in our active memory cache to perform the AI classification. Once the results are generated and returned to your browser, the images are purged from our live processing servers. We do not build permanent datasets from user uploads without explicit, opt-in consent.

3. Analytics and Tracking

We collect basic, anonymized telemetry data (such as browser type, general location, and interaction events) to help us improve the PawPrint UI and machine learning models. This data cannot be traced back to individual users.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide advanced insights, we utilize the Gemini AI framework. Data passed to Gemini is strictly related to the breed classification parameters and does not contain personal identifiable information (PII).

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at contact@pawprint.ai.
